Bayshore Village

Welcome to Bayshore Village a coastal-inspired rental home community where comfort, convenience, and relaxed living come together effortlessly. Nestled in Oak Island in Brunswick County, North Carolina, our newly built 1-, 2-, and 3-bedroom ranch-style homes and townhomes offer spacious layouts, modern finishes, and a serene atmosphere just minutes from Southport and Holden Beach. Here, you’ll enjoy the perfect blend of everyday ease and coastal charm — with private driveways, attached garages, and maintenance-free living that delivers the feel of a single-family home without the upkeep. Surrounded by beautiful scenery and resort-style amenities, Bayshore Village offers a tranquil, welcoming environment designed for work, play, and peaceful coastal living. Discover the Bayshore Village lifestyle today, where home feels like a getaway every day.
